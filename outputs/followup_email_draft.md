This is a follow-up to our communication dated 13 April 2026 regarding your department's metadata submission for the **UPPCL Consumer Billing Data** dataset (Submission ID: META-010, submitted on 9 April 2026).

As of today, we have not received a response or a revised submission from your department. We are writing again as the pending issues must be resolved before this submission can be approved and listed on the UP Metadata Platform.

**Issues Requiring Correction**

Our quality review identified two specific problems with submission META-010:

1. **Missing Data Owner Name**
   The `data_owner_name` field has been left blank. The SDA requires the name of a specific individual who is responsible for this dataset — not a designation or office name alone. Please provide the full name of the designated data owner (for example: "Shri Ravi Kumar, Manager MIS").

2. **Non-Standard Date Format in `last_updated` Field**
   The date entered is `30-11-2024` (DD-MM-YYYY format). The platform requires all dates to be submitted in **YYYY-MM-DD format**. The correct entry for this date should be `2024-11-30`.

**What Your Department Needs to Do**

Please make both corrections in the metadata submission form and resubmit by **5 May 2026**. If you require the original submission form or guidance on how to resubmit, please contact us and we will share the necessary materials.

We recognise that your department manages a large and important dataset, and we are committed to supporting you through the submission process. However, we are unable to approve or list this dataset on the platform until these corrections are made.

We look forward to receiving the revised submission by the date mentioned above.
